About

We are working with Microsoft and Clifford Chance to develop a system that offers artificial intelligent features to help lawyers to interpret law documents more quickly. The system need to be able to ingest law documents, group them into clusters and then identify the risk. We also work with team 39 and team 40 to split this challenging project into 3 parts - ingestion, grouping and presentation. We, team 38, are responsible to build a web app for the presentation of data and user feedback, that is, the 'learning' process of the system.
